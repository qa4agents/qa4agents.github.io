Call for Papers

Submission Types

QA4Agents welcomes two types of submissions: regular papers and presentation-only contributions.

Regular Papers

Regular papers are original research contributions addressing quality assurance of conversational agentic systems. Each submission will be reviewed by three members of the program committee. All accepted regular papers will be presented at the workshop by an author and published in the ISSRE Workshop Volume proceedings.

Regular papers include three paper categories:

  • Full Papers (8 pages) for mature research contributions presenting complete methodologies, experimental evaluations, and results.
  • Short Papers (4 pages) for preliminary results and emerging ideas not yet fully validated.
  • Datasets, Benchmarks & Evaluation Infrastructure Paper (4 pages) for datasets, benchmarks, and evaluation tools for conversational agentic systems.

All page limits include references and figures.

Presentation-Only Contributions

Presentation-only contributions consist of previously published submissions that will not appear in the workshop proceedings but are accepted for presentation to encourage discussion and networking among participants. Presentation-only contributions have no page limit and will be evaluated by the organizing committee.

Formatting Guidelines

All papers must be written in English and submitted as a single Portable Document Format (PDF) file with all fonts embedded. Submissions must adhere to the IEEE Computer Society Format Guidelines . Please use the same LaTeX/Word templates as the ISSRE Research Track: ISSRE 2026 Submission Info.
Submissions must be anonymous (double-blind review).
Please clearly indicate the type of your submission (Full paper, Short Paper, Datasets, Benchmarks & Evaluation Infrastructure Paper or Presentation-only Contribution) on the first page of the manuscript.

Submission & Registration

Papers must be submitted via EasyChair. At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference and present the work.
