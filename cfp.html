Call for Papers

Submission Types

QA4Agents welcomes two types of submissions: regular papers and presentation-only contributions.

Regular Papers

Regular papers are original research contributions addressing quality assurance of conversational agentic systems. Each submission will be reviewed by three members of the program committee. All accepted regular papers will be presented at the workshop by an author and published in the ISSRE Workshop Volume proceedings.

Regular papers include two paper categories:

  • Full Papers (8 pages) for mature research contributions presenting complete methodologies, experimental evaluations, and results.
  • Short Papers (4 pages) for preliminary results, ongoing works, emerging ideas not yet fully validated, datasets and benchmarks.

All page limits include references and figures.

Presentation-Only Contributions

The workshop also hosts presentation-only contributions describing previously published or already accepted papers closely related to the workshop topics. These contributions will not appear in the workshop proceedings but are accepted for presentation to encourage discussion, community building, and networking among participants. Authors are requested to submit the original paper, which will undergo a lightweight selection process focused on relevance and expected interest for the workshop audience. Presentation-only contributions have no page limit. All accepted presentation-only contributions will be presented at the workshop by an author.

Special Issue

Selected papers may be invited to submit an extended version to a scientific journal for possible inclusion in a special issue. Further details on potential journal collaborations will be announced later.

Formatting Guidelines

All papers must be written in English and submitted as a single Portable Document Format (PDF) file with all fonts embedded. Submissions must adhere to the IEEE Computer Society Format Guidelines . Please use the same LaTeX/Word templates as the ISSRE Research Track: ISSRE 2026 Submission Info.
Please clearly indicate the type of your submission (Full Paper, Short Paper, or Presentation-only Contribution) on the first page of the manuscript. For Presentation-only Contributions, indicate the original venue of publication.

Submission & Registration

Papers must be submitted via EasyChair. At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference and present the work.
